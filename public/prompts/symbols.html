  2. Generate them in order. Download each as 01.png, 02.png, … through 13.png (or just keep them in download-time order).
  3. Send me the batch back — I'll rename per the filenames shown and wire them into the app.
  4. Outputs should be square 512×512 PNG with transparent background wherever possible.
